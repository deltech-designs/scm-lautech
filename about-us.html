OUR MISSION, VISION AND VALUES

Who Are We?

A CHURCH FILLED WITH LOVE AND COMPASSION

We are a community bound together by God's love, committed to supporting each other through life's journey. Our church exists to provide a space where everyone, students, faculty, and families, can experience the transforming power of God's grace. We believe in serving others with compassion, uplifting each person's spirit, and making a positive impact in our university and beyond.

OUR HISTORY

Building a Church That Reflects God's Love and Grace

The Student Christian Movement (SCM) of Nigeria is the local expression of the World Student Christian Federation (WSCF), a Geneva-based non-governmental and non-profit making International Student Christian Organisation with 1508 associated and affiliated member Movement in 94 countries.

In early 1990s, Reverend Gomba F. Oyor, the National Travelling Secretary for the Western Region, tasked Jolly Ekpe, a corper Travelling Secretary based in Ife, with establishing an SCM chapter at the newly founded Oyo State University of Technology (LAUTECH).

Ekpe was supported by students from the SCM branch at Obafemi Awolowo University, including Prof. Olawale Olakunleyin. Within three months, they successfully completed the assignment, and the first SCM service at LAUTECH was held on January 13, 1991. Services have since been held in three locations: RCCG Jesus House Isale General with Bible Study held within campus, Mafowurosere Cooperative Building.
