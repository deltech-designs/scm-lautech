A Brief Background

The Student Christian Movement (SCM) of LAUTECH is a local chapter of the Student Christian Movement of Nigeria, the Nigeria expression of the World Student Christian Federation (WSCF).

The branch started 35 years ago by the effort of Elder John Ekpe, it had grown much more ever since into an active campus fellowship, participaring in outreaches within the state and amidst secondary school students. The fellowship places value on the Word of God and prayers as well as giving room to the Spirit of God in her meetings.

Today, we continue that legacy, honoring the foundations laid by our predecessors while giving room for growth and increase, as we aim to keep the mission to fill the earth with the glory of our God.
